About Us

Where Tradition Meets Innovation

At Bistro Bliss, we believe that dining is not just about the food; it's an experience, a moment to be cherished. Our culinary journey is crafted with passion, aiming to create an atmosphere where every moment spent with us is an opportunity to savor exceptional flavors and make lasting memories.

Our Philosophy: We take pride in offering a dining experience that goes beyond the ordinary. Our philosophy revolves around creating dishes that not only tantalize your taste buds but also tell a story. Each ingredient is carefully selected, and every dish is a manifestation of the dedication and expertise of our culinary team.

A Culinary Symphony: Step into our world, where dining becomes a sensory journey. Our menu is a carefully orchestrated symphony of flavors, textures, and aromas. From the first bite to the last, we aim to take you on a gastronomic adventure that transcends the ordinary.

Fresh and Local: We are committed to using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients. Our partnerships with local farmers and producers allow us to bring the best of the region to your plate. This commitment not only ensures quality but also supports our community.

Warm Ambiance: Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it's a welcoming space designed for you to relax and enjoy. The warm ambiance, attentive service, and thoughtfully curated surroundings create an inviting atmosphere for every occasion, from casual gatherings to special celebrations.

Food Menu

Nawabi Biryani

A Regal symphony of saffron-infused basmati rice and tender, marinated meat. This majestic dish promises a brief journey to the opulent era of Nawabi splendor, where each aromatic bite tells a tale of exquisite flavors and culinary elegance.

Price: ₹ 780

Mediterranean Bliss Platter

Grilled perfection meets a vibrant tapestry of sun-kissed flavors — juicy chicken, roasted veggies, and the Mediterranean's finest treasures. It's a symphony of taste, a dance of colors, and a journey to blissful indulgence, all in one delightful platter.

Price: ₹ 990

Baked Ziti

Ziti pasta, cooked to perfection, is enveloped in a robust marinara sauce, its tomato richness complemented by aromatic herbs. The pasta is generously coated in a velvety blend of creamy ricotta and melted mozzarella, creating a harmonious medley of textures.

Price: ₹ 650

Pav Bhaji

Delectable dish features a spiced and flavorful vegetable mash, comprising potatoes, peas, tomatoes, and a melange of aromatic spices. The medley is expertly simmered to perfection and served with soft, buttered pav (bread rolls).

Price: ₹ 460

Citrus Serenity Fusion

Culinary masterpiece that brings together the zesty vibrancy of citrus fruits with the tranquil allure of serene flavors. Imagine a symphony of tastes where the bright notes of oranges, lemons, or limes dance harmoniously with an array of complementary ingredients.

Price: ₹ 500

Mystic Ricotta Ravioli Delight

Small, delicate parcels consist of thin pasta dough enveloping a delectable filling, often featuring ricotta cheese, spinach, meat, or a combination of flavors.

Price: ₹ 650
